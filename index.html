About

I'm a high school student from the Waikato region of New Zealand, with a passion for wildlife photography. I specialise in capturing native and rare New Zealand birds, often pushing myself further than I can handel with new and creative photo ideas. I'm excited to continue honing my skills while documenting New Zealand's vast biodiversity.
