About

I'm a high school student from the Waikato region of New Zealand, with a passion for wildlife photography. I specialise in capturing native and rare New Zealand birds, often pushing myself further than I can handle with new and creative photo ideas. I'm excited to continue developing my skills while documenting New Zealand's biodiversity.

Contact

Email: matai@moorfield.co.nz

While I do specialise in wildlife photography, I am also skilled in other types, such as portraits and events. I charge a small hourly rate to cover costs for my gear and really enjoy capturing new and unique opportunities in photographs. I'd like to have a chance to develop my skills. For inquiries, please contact me.
