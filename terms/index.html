TERMS

SECURITY

Our shopping cart is secure and protected by SSL encryption technology. SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) is a method of ensuring that information submitted through your web site is secure and cannot be accessed by unauthorised users. When a site offers an SSL-secured form, the information submitted via that form (typically credit card information) is encrypted using a special certificate key and then decrypted with another key after it has been transmitted. When you access a site secured with SSL using your browser, a symbol displays in the browser window indicating that the site is secure.

RETURNS

Sometimes, a product might not be quite what you wanted, or is faulty or damaged. We get that, which is why we accept returns.


Change of Mind

If you change your mind and would like to return a product with 14 days of receiving your order, please follow the returns process below. The item must be in saleable and undamaged condition, and in its original packaging. You will need to cover the cost of shipping the item back to us, but there is no restocking fee. The return of ingredients will be dealt with on a case-by-case basis.


Damaged in Transit or Faulty Products

If any products arrive damaged, please notify us within 24 hours. For both damaged and faulty products, please complete the returns process below. We will likely need more information, or photos or videos, but we will discuss this with you after the request has been received. Matai Moorfield will arrange the shipping of the item back to us at Matai Moorfield's cost.


Returns Process

For all types of product returns, please complete the following process:

1. Go to www.matai.moorfield.co.nz and find the contact form

2. Email the address displayed or use the contact form

3. Please provide what you ordered and when in the discription/message

The return request will be processed by staff and actioned or we may contact you if further information is required.


RECEIVING A REFUND

Once your goods have been returned to us, and passed quality checks, we will process your refund request. Your refund will either be processed in the original payment format (i.e. credit card) or as store credit, depending on your selection, within 30 days of the initial return request. All returned products will be credited to the original purchaser including GST but excluding courier charges. Credit card refunds can take up to five days after actioning before you receive it.
